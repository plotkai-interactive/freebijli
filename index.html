What we do?

At Free Bijli, we simplify your transition to solar energy. We begin by assessing your residential or commercial electricity needs and educating you on choosing the optimal solar panel setup for your situation. Our experts provide personalized recommendations based on your energy usage and financial goals.

We then connect you with trusted installers and manage the entire installation process, ensuring a seamless experience. Additionally, we offer tailored financing options that align with your budget, making solar energy both affordable and accessible. Our comprehensive approach ensures that you understand every step and benefit from a hassle-free transition to clean, renewable energy.
